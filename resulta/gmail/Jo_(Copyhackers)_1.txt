negative voices end up influencing what you write?

copywriter for QuickBooks, I had

and 

2) only really heard about the troubles users had with it. It didn't
matter that QuickBooks had revolutionized accounting software and was
actually extraordinarily easy to use.

I would often catch myself starting from a place of negativity. As in,
"sure i'll try to sell this software but man are entrepreneurs
struggling with it."

I had to STOP myself from thinking that way.

And I eventually had to stop myself from looking at 1-star reviews. 

Because thinking about the naysayers and Negative Nellies in the room
will NOT help you sell a product.

objections in your copy.

that out of the gate.)

happiest prospect in the room. Write for the person who's excited about
what you've got.
